ABOUT US

BUTCHER BINS LLP is a specialized chicken rendering facility dedicated to the efficient and sustainable recycling of chicken by-products. We process waste from poultry into fine particles, ultimately creating high-quality powdered forms suitable for animal feed. Our state-of-the-art machinery ensures minimal waste, contributing to a cleaner environment while providing a valuable resource for animal nutrition.

Although we don’t handle the packaging and selling of our final products, our commitment to eco-friendly practices and quality processing stands strong. At Butcher Bins LLP, we believe in the future of responsible waste management.
